Key Finding: Compound Decline Drives Risk

Individual dimension correlations with risk tier are weak (0.05 – 0.07 Spearman). But the derived compound features show much stronger signal: critical_cval_count correlates at 0.67 and total_declining_count at 0.41. This confirms that complication risk is driven by multi-dimensional deterioration, not single-factor decline.
